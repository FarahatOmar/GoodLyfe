What should I bring to my first session?

For your first session, bring comfortable workout attire, a water bottle, and a positive attitude!

Are there locker facilities available?

Yes, we provide locker rooms with secure storage for your belongings.

Can I bring a friend to a class?

Of course! Members can bring a friend to try out a class for free.

What's your cancellation policy for classes?

We require at least 24 hours notice for class cancellations.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we have experienced personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions.

Are there age restrictions for classes?

Most classes are open to adults of all ages, but some may have age restrictions.

Is there a trial period for memberships?

Yes, we offer a 7-day trial period for our monthly memberships.

What's the schedule for group classes?

You can find the class schedule on our website's "Classes" page.

Can I pause or cancel my membership?

Memberships can be paused or canceled according to our membership terms.

What happens if I miss a class I signed up for?

If you miss a class, the spot will be forfeited, but you can sign up for another class.

Do you provide dietary guidance?

While we don't offer full nutrition plans, our trainers can provide general dietary advice.
